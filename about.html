About Me

Nice to meet you!

My name is Jenny Noh, and I'm an undergraduate Informatics student specializing in human-computer interaction at University of California, Irvine. Alongside my studies, I run a stationery business that combines cute animal characters representing MBTI personalities.

I'm a dedicated and fast learner, constantly seeking new challenges and growth opportunities. With a startup mindset, I love pushing boundaries and thinking creatively.

As an aspiring UX/UI designer, I'm passionate about blending technology with vibrant colors to create immersive experiences. My goal is to infuse a spectrum of colors into the lives of people, while ensuring inclusivity and representation. I strongly believe that design should bridge gaps and resonate with diverse perspectives.
